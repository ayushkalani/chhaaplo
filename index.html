WELCOME TO CHHAAPLO

ChhapLo India Private Ltd(chhaaplo.in)a service provider company was founded with an aim to minimize the gap between unnecessary groundwork and useful technology.This lack of utility made us think the possibility of providing service or rather contributing to your personal work by giving you a helping hand and printing your documents and delivering it on time without the hussle and tussle of going to printing shops and waiting for hours. We understand the gravity of the situation when the deadlines are approaching but the bhaiyas and aunties just evaporate the situation by making you wait.We believe that wasting money and time while your precious life stands on the edge is something to care about and thus we envision a platform where everyone has access to upload documents at their own comfort and get the delivery at nearby locations. We aim to create resources that can help aid students and professionals in keeping themselves up to date with their ongoing competitive lifestyle.
Every year, there are over 1000 colleges hosting on an average of 10 events. Every event collectively leads to around 1000 printing material which needs to be continously modified and thus printing them takes utmost care. At this current stage, Around Rs.5000 is wasted on every event. Needless to say that it is a big loss for the events and big gains for the printing shops. We give you the power to make last minute changes and then print whenever you are absolutely confident. Proffessionals and students also face problems when their deadlines are nearing and they want printed documents as early as possible but have to go through the rigorous process of visiting cyber cafes and printing shops. After facing such problems ourselves , we have made a product to facilitate your work and give you easy printing solutions.
