‘We don't provide service, we contribute to your work.’

In this era of escalating and never ending competition, there arises a growing need to get important documents on time. Maintaining a repository of these important documents is an arduos task and lack of these prove to be very hefty.

Chhaaplo was created with the desire to solve this enormous but petty problem. We believe that we can contribute to the work of our customers by facilitating their work and providing documents on time without having to go to shops and standing in queues for hours. We believe in solving problems and this has been an effort from our side to solve this daily problem.Every year, there are about 1000 events in which aproximately 1500 total pages are printed. We allow you to make spontaneous changes in your documents and updating them without wasting money by printing unnecessary pages.
